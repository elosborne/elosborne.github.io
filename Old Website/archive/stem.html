STEM Ambassador

www.stemnet.org.uk/ambassadors/

I am a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) ambassador. As a STEM ambassador, I work with teachers and schools to inspire students to become the next generation of scientists. I do this in a variety of ways, such as 'meet the scientist' events. This involves me visiting schools and doing workshops or demonstrations, chatting to students via the internet and discussing potential career options. I also interact with young people through scientific engagement events such as: science fairs and university open days.
