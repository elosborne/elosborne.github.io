Cheltenham Science Festival 2017

www.cheltenhamfestivals.com

This year I went to Cheltenham science festival for the first time. I had an absolutely amazing time! The festival has loads of amazing talks demonstrations and plenty of activities so that you can join in all of the fun. I was involved in The MakerShack and University of Southampton's researchers cafe. In the MakerShack I ran a comet making station. This was great fun as I got to play with dry ice all day. Dry ice creates lots of steam which certainly helped draw in a big crowd. Cometeers who visited the station helped me make the comets and learnt all about what comets are made from and how they get their distinctive tail.

Researchers cafe is a regular event usually held in Southampton, but this year the University of Southampton Roadshow took the researchers cafe along with them. This event is very informal. People are invited to chat with researchers about their work over a cup of coffee and maybe even a cake!
